Profile
======
Economics PhD candidate in empirical economics and political economy, with a research agenda centered on using granular open data across space, time, and behavior to study institutions, governance, and decision-making.

Research Interests and Methods
======
- **Fields:** Political economy; institutions and governance; applied economics; computational social science.
- **Methods:** Causal inference; panel data econometrics; GIS; remote sensing and night lights; administrative data; large-scale behavioral data; algorithmic benchmarking.

Research and Applied Data Experience
======

- **Queensland Centre for Population Research (School of the Environment, UQ)**, Brisbane.  
  *Senior Research Assistant*, 2022-2025.
  - Developed a reproducible demographic estimation pipeline for the Queensland Government Statistical Office (QGSO), producing small-area (SA2) population estimates with a focus on Indigenous populations.
  - Implemented a modified IPF procedure for non-full-dimensional constraint structures.
  - Designed a novel integerisation algorithm converting fractional IPF outputs into integer population counts.
  - Liaised with QGSO stakeholders and authored technical documentation and handover materials to support maintenance, auditability, and reuse.

- **UQ International Comparisons Database (UQICD)**, Brisbane.  
  *Senior Research Assistant*, 2022-2025.
  - Refactored and documented the core R codebase supporting UQICD indicators and workflows, improving maintainability and reproducibility.
  - Built data pipelines enabling user-defined regional aggregations for purchasing power parity comparisons and developed publicly available dashboards in R.
  - Managed acquisition, cleaning, harmonisation, and validation of heterogeneous international datasets to support stable updates.

- **Real Estate and Transport Project (Prof. Alicia Rambaldi)**, Brisbane.  
  *Senior Research Assistant*, 2021-2022.
  - Acquired and cleaned large administrative property transaction data in collaboration with the NSW Valuer General.
  - Integrated sales records with GTFS transport networks and GIS layers, constructing accessibility measures for econometric modelling.
  - Produced distance-weighted and route-diversity indices to quantify public transport access in commercial property analysis.

- **Ibn Haldun University**, Istanbul, Turkey.  
  *Research Assistant*, 2018-2021.
  - Built text analytics and sentiment analysis workflows for social media data (Twitter API), including data collection, cleaning, and visualization.
  - Supported faculty research projects.

- **Penn State University**, State College, USA.  
  *Research Assistant (Prof. Bill Goffe; Prof. Vijay Krishna)*, 2010-2017.
  - Developed a Python tool to flag irregularities in assessment data, automating detection of potential academic misconduct.
  - Contributed proofs for research on voting rules and welfare properties.

- **Koc University (Engineering)**, Istanbul, Turkey.  
  *Research Assistant*, 2004-2008.
  - Applied pattern recognition methods to sensor data for driver identification (DRIVE-SAFE project).
  - Implemented video deinterlacing algorithms for broadcast enhancement and signal-processing applications.
